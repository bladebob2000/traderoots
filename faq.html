Can I Cancel a Trade?

You can cancel trades without consequence at least 12 hours in advance. If cancelled within 12 hours, the other user may mark it as a late-notice cancellation.

Two or more late-notice cancellations within one month will be visible on your profile. You may appeal a mark by contacting support.
