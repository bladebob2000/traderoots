Our Roots Run Deep

We’re building communities through skill exchange, one connection at a time.

Our Story

Trade Roots was born from a simple observation: many people can’t access services they need due to financial constraints, despite having skills to offer. Trade Roots enables service exchange without money as a barrier, strengthening communities through mutual support.

Founded in 2025

Our Mission

To create a world where everyone can access the services they need, regardless of financial status, by facilitating skill exchanges that strengthen community bonds.

When we lift each other up, we all rise together.
